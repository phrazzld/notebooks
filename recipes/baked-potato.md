# Baked Potato

## Ingredients

-   Russet potato
-   Butter
-   Coarse Kosher salt
-   Freshly-ground black pepper

## Directions

1. Heat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Prepare your baking sheet.
3. Poke your potato all over with a fork.
4. Bake for 25 minutes.
5. Brush the potato with melted butter. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
6. Turn over and bake for 20 minutes.
7. Gently squeeze to check for doneness.
8. Slice, squeeze, and serve.
